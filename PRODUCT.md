# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Quantitative analysts and portfolio managers running optimization and walk-forward backtests on equity portfolios. Used at a desktop in an analytical workflow — not a mobile/tablet surface.

## Product Purpose

Portfolio Lab combines a portfolio optimizer (efficient frontier, Sharpe/Sortino/CVaR objectives) with a walk-forward backtester. Users pick tickers, a date range, and an objective, hit Run, and get optimization results (weights, frontier) alongside out-of-sample backtest performance (equity curve, drawdown, risk metrics). Success means results are legible at a glance and the tool gets out of the way.

## Brand Personality

Precise, professional, minimal. The terminal-native fintech tool that respects the user's time.

## Anti-references

- Bloomberg Terminal visual density (too noisy)
- Robinhood/retail fintech (too bright, too casual)
- Generic Dash/Bootstrap dashboards with default blue buttons and card grids

## Design Principles

1. Data is the hero — chrome should disappear, numbers should stand out
2. Dark sidebar, light canvas — controls recede, results breathe
3. Monospace for numbers, system sans for labels — clear register separation
4. Contrast before aesthetics — every text element earns its place at WCAG AA minimum

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. All text at 4.5:1 against its background. Reduced-motion respected where animations are added.
